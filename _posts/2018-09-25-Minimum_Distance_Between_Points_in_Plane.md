### Q:
Given a finite set $$P$$ of points in $$\mathbb{R}^2$$, construct

1. a $$O(n^2)$$ algorithm

2. a $$O(n \log n)$$ algorithm

3. a $$O(n)$$ algorithm

for finding two points $$x, y \in P$$ such that $$\left\lVert x-y \right\lVert$$ is minimized.
